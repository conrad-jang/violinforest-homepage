history_edu About Me & Career

A distinguished graduate of the Seoul National University College of Music, Director Eunbi Cha has dedicated her life to the mastery of the violin.

With over 50+ solo and orchestral performances across Europe and Asia, her technical precision is matched only by her artistic soul. She has served as a guest concertmaster for multiple prestigious youth orchestras and is a recognized member of the Classical Educators Association.
